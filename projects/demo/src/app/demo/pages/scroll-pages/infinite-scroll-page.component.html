Infinite scroll

pa-infinite-scroll is a wrapper component using intersection observer to setup a performant way for loading more data when reaching anchors. One anchor is positioned in the middle of the container, and the other is after the content.

By default, the intersection observer is relative to the viewport. It becomes relative to the host when the host has overflow: auto; or overflow-y: scroll; style property set.
