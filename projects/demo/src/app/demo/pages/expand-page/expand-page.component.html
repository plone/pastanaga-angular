Expand

Expand component allows to expand/collapse a section by clicking on its header or on the chevron button next to it.

PaExpandModule provide different directives to be used with pa-expand:

  • pa-expand-header: its content is displayed next to the chevron button, clicking on it toggles the expand
  • pa-expand-body: its content is displayed below the expand header. Visible only when expanded.
  • pa-expand-header-side-block: its content is displayed next to the pa-expand-header. Clicking on it doesn't toggle the expand.
