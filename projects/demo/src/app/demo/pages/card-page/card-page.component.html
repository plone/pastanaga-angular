Card

Pastanaga provides a generic card wrapper component managing card style like border, padding, state (hover/focus/disabled).

Card content is injected through a ng-content

The card itself doesn't manage its own size: it will take the available size depending on its content. The card container is responsible for managing the card size depending on the needs.

Disabled card

This card is disabled, clicking on it will not trigger cardClick event.

Inputs

disabled
Disable the card, meaning clicking on the card will do nothing. Optional

Outputs

cardClick
Event emitted when clicking on an enabled card, and when pressing enter on an enabled and focused card. It contains the MouseEvent or KeyboardEvent triggered.
